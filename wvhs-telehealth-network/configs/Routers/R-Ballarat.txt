! ACCESS CONTROL LIST — ACL 100
! Purpose: Block Administration subnet from accessing Radiology
! Implements: Principle of Least Privilege

!   /28 subnet mask 255.255.255.240 → wildcard 0.0.0.15
!   0 bits = must match exactly, 1 bits = don't care

!   (filter at source — before routing resources are wasted)

access-list 100 permit ip any any
